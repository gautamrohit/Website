    THE ADMISSION PROCEDURE

    The admission procedure for the various programmes at NITs ensures that only the best of students make it to their campuses. The admissions are carried out through the following highly competitive examinations. JEE (Mains) Previously All India Engineering Entrance Examination (AIEEE) Direct Admission of Students Abroad (DASA) Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) Admission to the Doctoral Programme (Ph.D)
